How AVO Works

Simple, secure, and transparent data sharing in four easy steps

1

Connect Your Wallet

Link your Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.) to authenticate your identity. Your wallet address becomes your secure identifier on the blockchain.

2

Upload & Encrypt

Select your files and upload them. AVO automatically encrypts your data using military-grade encryption before it ever leaves your device. You remain in full control.

3

Grant Access

Choose who can view your files by entering their wallet addresses. Smart contracts on Polygon blockchain verify and manage all access permissions automatically.

4

Track & Manage

Monitor all file access in real-time with blockchain-verified logs. Revoke permissions instantly whenever needed. Your data, your rules.
